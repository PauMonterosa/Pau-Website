## âš¡ The Innovation: "Adaptive Light-Shielding"

What sets **TakeDPulse** apart from standard student projects is its **innovative optical isolation architecture**. 

Unlike generic sensors that suffer from ambient noise, we developed a **custom 3D-printed biometric chassis** designed with internal light-trapping geometry. This ensures that the LED-Phototransistor pair operates in a controlled environment, maximizing the Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR) and allowing for stable readings even in high-interference environments.
